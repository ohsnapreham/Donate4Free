What D4F does

Raising Awareness

Raising awareness for donations to children's and refugees. Also providing reliable links for private donations.

Free Donation

Creating monetized content on YouTube to generate revenue to donate. Instead of donating with actual cash, we help you donate with your time.

Creating Opportunities

We are also open to accepting creative contents from others for which they will be credited for. This creates an opportunity for other content creators to gain YT exposures.

Who? Why? Where?

Non-profit Organization

My name is Hossain Muhaymin. I am junior at Boston College majoring in Computer Science and minoring in Finance.

I have always had the urge to make humanitarian efforts. During the COVID-19 pandemic, I started Donate4Free. Gaining audience motivated me to create more content, which led the channel to be monetized. And that's when the donation process started. 100% of the revenue is donated to charity.

Donate4Free was started in NYC on June 13th, 2020. As of September 2021, the channel holds 500k+ views.

Some of my work

The video contents often are vlogs and fun-fact videos. Occasionally, I upload contents from my subscribers and credit them to promote their work, while earning revenue for donation.
